Forum Rules

1. General Conduct

  • Be respectful to all community members
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Keep discussions family-friendly
  • No spamming or excessive self-promotion

2. Content Guidelines

  • Stay on topic in each forum section
  • No explicit or inappropriate content
  • Don't share personal information
  • Use appropriate language

3. Posting Rules

  • Search before creating new topics
  • Use descriptive titles
  • Post in the correct section
  • No duplicate posts

4. Account Rules

  • One account per person
  • No account sharing
  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Use an appropriate username

5. Moderation

  • Moderators' decisions are final
  • Report rule violations instead of confronting users
  • No ban evasion
  • Follow moderator instructions
