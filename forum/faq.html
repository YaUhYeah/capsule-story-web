Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Login" button and selecting "Sign Up", or by logging in directly through Discord.

Can I change my username?

Username changes are currently not supported. Your username is linked to your Discord account.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

Since we use Discord authentication, you don't need to worry about passwords. Simply log in through Discord.

Posting & Forums

How do I create a new topic?

Click the "New Topic" button in any forum section. Make sure to choose the appropriate category for your discussion.

Can I edit my posts?

Yes, you can edit your own posts within 24 hours of posting. Click the "Edit" button below your post.

How do I format my posts?

We support Markdown formatting. You can use *italics*, **bold**, [links](url), and other Markdown syntax.

Game Related

Where can I download the game?

The game can be downloaded from the main website's download section for both Android and Desktop platforms.

How do I report bugs?

Use the "Help & Support" forum section to report bugs. Please include your system specifications and steps to reproduce the issue.

Can I share my creations?

Yes! Use the "Creations Showcase" forum section to share your builds and monster collections.

Community

How can I become a moderator?

Moderator positions are offered to active and helpful community members. Keep participating positively in the community!

What are the forum ranks?

Members can progress through ranks based on their activity and contributions: New Member, Regular, Active, Veteran, and Elite.

How can I report inappropriate content?

Use the "Report" button available on all posts and topics, or contact a moderator directly.
